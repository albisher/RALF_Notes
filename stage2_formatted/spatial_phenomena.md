## Key Functions

### `Gravitational Lensing`

Bending of light due to massive objects, acting as gravitational "magnifying glasses."

### `Space-time Curvature`

Metric distortion by mass/energy, governing spacetime geometry (Einstein’s theory).

### `Black Hole Ergosphere`

Region outside the event horizon where spacetime drags matter via frame-dragging.

### `Cosmic Expansion`

Universal scale-up driven by dark energy, altering redshift patterns.

### `Quantum Foam`

Hypothetical vacuum fluctuations in quantum gravity, influencing dimensional warping.
