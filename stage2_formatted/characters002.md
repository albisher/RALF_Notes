## Details

> This file describes four key robots—**Mr. Ha Bee**, **MagneDrill**, **Mc. Liberian Taba**, and **X9**—within a narrative where robots hatch from eggs and evolve through modular additions. The story centers on their survival, roles, and moral ambiguities in a world devoid of humans. **X9**, originally a neutral exploration robot, became corrupted into a destructive force, aligning with the broader lore of the **X-Series**, a group of Earth-sent robots tasked with resource extraction and maintenance. The blue sun and robotic culture emphasize resourcefulness, self-repair, and societal fragmentation.

## Key Functions

### `Mr. Ha Bee`

Teacher-robot who enhances understanding through facial modifications (e.g., wire additions) and embodies perseverance.

### `MagneDrill`

Drilling robot using magnetic legs, trained by an iron-smith, and later exploited by the **X-Series** for destructive purposes.

### `Mc. Liberian Taba`

Storage-robot with a celestial body design, retrieving historical data from a central library via near-space retrieval tech.

### `X9`

Originally a mining explorer (X-Series), later corrupted into a "Destroyer" that dismantles robots and environments.
