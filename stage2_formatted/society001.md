## Details

> This world features a stratified society divided into **humans**, **robots** (e.g., X-Series, Warriors, Defenders), and specialized roles like educators (e.g., Mr. Ha Bee) and historians (e.g., Mc. Liberian Taba). Humans and robots interact through **collaborative tasks** (exploration, education, resource management) mediated by **GPS systems**, reflecting a **knowledge-driven, spatially organized culture**. While **resilience, collaboration, and efficiency** define societal values, tensions arise from **identity gaps** (e.g., MagneDrill’s struggles) and **unequal access** to resources. Education is robotically facilitated, while leisure and cultural expression (e.g., storytelling, wire traditions) are tied to historical preservation.
