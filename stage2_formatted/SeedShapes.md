## Details

> This file enumerates distinct morphological terms describing seed and leaf shapes, useful for botanical classification, plant identification, or artistic/design inspiration. The terms cover a range of geometric and organic forms, from symmetrical (e.g., *oval*, *square*) to asymmetrical (e.g., *flipped heart*, *flipped triangle*). Some entries (e.g., *needle-like*, *spring-like*) imply elongated or dynamic traits, while others (e.g., *palmate*, *fan-shaped*) suggest branching or spreading patterns.
> 
> The list serves as a reference for researchers, educators, or designers working with natural forms, particularly in fields like taxonomy, horticulture, or bio-inspired design.
