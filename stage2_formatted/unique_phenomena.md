## Details

> This file enumerates unusual cosmic events and effects, including theoretical and observed anomalies like temporal distortions, quantum fluctuations in extreme environments, and extreme gravitational phenomena. These phenomena are studied in astrophysics to understand fundamental physics beyond classical models, such as general relativity and quantum mechanics.
> 
> The list covers:
> - **Temporal anomalies** (e.g., time dilation zones, wormholes, black holes).
> - **Quantum and energy-related effects** (e.g., plasma rivers, gamma-ray bursts).
> - **Gravitational and relativistic phenomena** (e.g., gravity wells, dark matter/dark energy influences).
> - **Interstellar and galactic-scale observations** (e.g., cosmic rays, interstellar medium).
