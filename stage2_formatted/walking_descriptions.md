## Key Functions

### `Sturdy legs`

Provides balanced walking stability under normal conditions.

### `Mechanical legs`

Offers enhanced durability and repeatability in controlled environments.

### `Insect-like legs`

Mimics natural insect locomotion for rough, uneven terrain traversal.

### `Flexible legs`

Enables climbing obstacles by adapting to irregular surfaces.

### `Telescopic leg`

Allows precise adjustments for fine motor tasks or vertical positioning.

### `Spring-loaded legs`

Facilitates jumping over gaps or uneven obstacles via energy storage.

### `Padded legs`

Reduces noise and vibration for stealthy or low-impact movement.

### `Articulated legs`

Accommodates uneven surfaces by adjusting joint angles dynamically.
