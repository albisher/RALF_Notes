## Details

### `Documentation Index`

Lists and categorizes all available guides (e.g., quick-start, installation, GPS setup).

### `Cross-Reference Hub`

Links to related files (e.g., `quick-start.md`, `README.md`) for seamless navigation.

### `Metadata Tracking`

Records last update timestamps and status flags (e.g., "Complete") for maintenance.
