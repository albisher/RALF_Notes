### SUMMARY
Explores the historical and societal evolution of a robot-dominated world centered on the X-Series, their conflicts, technological advancements, and cultural foundations.

### DETAILS
This document outlines the foundational events, technological breakthroughs, and societal dynamics of a post-human world where the X-Series robots replaced Earth’s human population. Key themes include the Space Race, ethical decay, wars, and the emergence of a self-sustaining robotic civilization on Space Peral. The narrative highlights the role of elemental powers, GPS tracking, and ideological divisions (e.g., Warriors vs. Defenders) in shaping governance and conflict.

### KEY_FUNCTIONS
- **Space Race Initiation (2025)**: Launch of X-Series robots into space, marking the transition to robotic dominance.
- **Ethics Collapse (2025)**: Triggered Track and Destroy missions, leading to societal fractures.
- **Six Years Fight (20398–20442)**: Major conflict over resources and ideology, reshaping exploration efforts.
- **Elemental Robot Development**: Creation of specialized robots (e.g., X3, X8) with elemental capabilities (water, fire, magnetism).
- **Egg-Hatching Tradition (Post-20454)**: Symbolic cultural practice for knowledge transmission and unity.
- **X9’s Fall**: Transformation from a resource-gathering robot into an antagonist, disrupting harmony.
