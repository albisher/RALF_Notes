### SUMMARY
Document outlining security and privacy concerns for a creative application, focusing on data handling, AI interactions, authentication, and ownership policies.

### DETAILS
This document outlines critical security and privacy considerations for a creative application, addressing sensitive content handling, AI service interactions, user authentication, and data ownership. It maps out concerns around data privacy, encryption, anonymization, and access control to ensure compliance with user expectations and regulatory standards.

### KEY_FUNCTIONS
- **Data Privacy Assessment**: Evaluates sensitivity of creative content and privacy needs for stories/worlds.
- **AI Service Safeguards**: Defines anonymization, credential protection, and logging policies for AI interactions.
- **User Authentication Framework**: Specifies account requirements, access control, and guest/demo options.
- **Data Ownership & Licensing**: Establishes ownership rules, derivative work policies, and attribution mechanisms.

### DEPENDENCIES
none

### USAGE
This document serves as a checklist for defining privacy/security policies in a creative application. Key stakeholders should review and refine answers to align with legal, ethical, and functional requirements.

### CALLOUTS
>[!INFO]- Important Note
> **Critical Focus Areas**: Prioritize encryption for sensitive data and enforce strict credential management to prevent unauthorized access.
> **User Trust**: Transparency in data handling (e.g., logging policies) builds trust but requires balancing with privacy needs.
>[!WARNING]- Caution
> **AI Risks**: Unsanitized data sent to AI services could expose creative work to unintended exposure or misuse. Implement strict sanitization workflows.
> **Legal Compliance**: Ensure policies align with GDPR/CCPA (or local laws) for data ownership and user consent.
