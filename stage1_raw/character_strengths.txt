### SUMMARY
Lists key character strengths and traits for personal and professional development.

### DETAILS
This file enumerates a curated set of **100+ intrinsic human qualities**, categorized under broad themes like resilience, cognitive skills, emotional intelligence, and behavioral traits. These traits are often studied in psychology (e.g., VIA Character Strengths Inventory) to assess strengths for growth, leadership, or self-improvement. The list serves as a foundational reference for identifying, developing, or recognizing strengths in individuals or teams.
