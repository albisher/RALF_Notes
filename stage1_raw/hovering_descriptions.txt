- **Complex route navigation**: Dynamically adapts to unpredictable aerial paths.
- **Precise altitude control**: Ensures stability via automated altitude regulation.
- **Silent operation**: Minimizes noise for stealth or environmental compliance.
- **Obstacle avoidance**: Detects and evades obstacles in real-time.
