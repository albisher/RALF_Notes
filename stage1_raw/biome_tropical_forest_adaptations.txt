- **Symbiosis with Fauna**: Mutualistic partnerships (e.g., mycorrhizal fungi, seed dispersal by animals).

- **Adhesion Mechanisms**: Sticky surfaces (e.g., *Dipterocarps*) for seed dispersal and climbing vines.
